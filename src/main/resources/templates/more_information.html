The application’s aim is to implement the issuance of a marriage dissolution certificate. All participants that are being involved in the divorce must have been registered in our site. If you are:

  1. Lawyer
    • Commencement of divorce proceedings

      Give the required information for the other lawyer, notary and the Spouses(with AFM/AMKA and last name) by pressing insert member’s names and then by pressing insert document’s details to add the divorce’s details.

      Confirm your devorce

      Press confirmation of devorce and then approval.

      Cancel your devorce

      Press cancellation of devorce and then confirmed

  2. Spouse
    • Confirm your devorce

      Press confirmation of devorce and then approval.

  3. Notary
    • Fill in the account’s details

      Press insert notarial instrument and fill in the required documents.
